## 项目介绍

极简翻译是一个面向网页阅读场景的 Chrome 翻译扩展。

用户在任何网页上选中单词或句子后，即可在对应正文位置下直接看到翻译结果，而不需要复制文本、切换页面或打开独立翻译站点。插件支持多翻译源聚合、英文单词词典信息、以及 TTS 朗读能力，适合日常阅读英文文章、技术文档、资讯网站和跨语言内容。

## 核心卖点

- 划词即译，减少页面切换
- 支持单词和整句翻译
- 聚合多个翻译源结果
- 英文单词支持音标、词性、释义、例句
- 支持 TTS 朗读与音色设置
- 支持扩展商业翻译接口配置

## 商店简短描述

Select a word or sentence on any webpage and translate it instantly with voice playback and dictionary details.

## 商店详细描述

Minimal Translation is a Chrome extension designed for fast in-page translation.

Select a word or sentence on any webpage and the extension inserts a compact translation block directly under the related content. It helps you stay focused while reading articles, documentation, blogs, and bilingual content without switching tabs.

Key features:

- Translate selected words and sentences instantly
- View results from multiple translation providers
- Show phonetics, parts of speech, definitions, and examples for English words
- Listen to the original text with built-in TTS playback
- Choose different voice styles and tune speech rate and pitch
- Configure an external commercial translation endpoint for expanded translation options

This extension is suitable for language learners, developers, students, and anyone who reads foreign-language webpages regularly.
